📱 Step 1 — Record Your Video

Follow these tips before you press record. Better video = better feedback!

📐

Film from the SIDE

Stand at 90° to the player — a perfect side view, not front or back.

✅ Side view ❌ Front view
📏

Stand 4–6 Metres Away

Far enough to see the whole body — head to feet — with a bit of space around them.

Roughly 5 big steps away
📸

Hip-Height Camera

Hold your phone at hip / waist height — not at your face and not on the ground.

Same height as player's hips
👁️

Full Body in Frame

Both the player's HEAD and FEET must be visible at all times. Don't zoom in.

Head + feet always visible
☀️

Good Lighting

Outdoors during the day is best. Avoid dark rooms or filming against bright sun.

✅ Outdoors ❌ Dark room
🎬

Capture the Full Action

Start recording BEFORE the action begins. Film at least 5 seconds. Keep the phone still — no panning!

Minimum 5 seconds
